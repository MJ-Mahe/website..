Caprese salad

Ingredients:

3 ripe vine tomatoes (each about the same size as the mozzarella ball), room temperature, sliced
250g mozzarella in water, drained and sliced
a handful of leaves basil, torn
2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Method:

STEP 1:Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices over a plate or platter. Tear over the basil, season with salt and pepper, then drizzle over the oil and balsamic vinegar. Serve at room temperature.
